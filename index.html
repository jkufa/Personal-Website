Past

As an Electrical Member, I got my first hands on experience with PCB cad software, designing & assemblying 2 unique boards. I also got to do some mechanical work, designing a gimbal mount for one of our cameras.

My time as Vice President was a bit more free form. I took on the goals of expanding our communication structure, engaging new members, and improving our member retention rate. In this time, I worked creating a Discord Bot capable of maintaining and assigning user roles in our Discord Server.

Being competition lead put me in charge of one main thing: Ensuring the team made it to competition. I was responsible for forwaring competition manual changes, and keeping track of our internal deadlines to ensure we're on track to complete everything in time.

DoubleCross

Past, Hackathon

DoubleCross is a Mafia style game, where one person, the double agent, has to make it to the end of the game without being caught. One person, the operative, knows who the double agent is, but can't explicitly tell! Instead, the operative runs through various mini challenges such as a drawing challenge and charades, to let the civilians figure out who the double agent is.

The cool part about this game is the integration with the Twilio API. The operative will recieve a special phone call that contains a special keyword that reveals who the double agent is. The double agent will receive a text message with this same keyword.

I was in charge of setting up or project's architecture: an ASP.Net MVC web application. Using cshtml, a little bit of javascript, and C#, I also created the main front end & controllers to tie together the front and back end. I was also in charge of integrating everyone's features together.

DoubleCross won TigerHack's Best Use of Twilio Award.

Pokemon Team Generator

Future, Personal

Pokemon Team Generator is an application configured to help with VGC teambuilding. It will create a full party of 6 Pokemon to be used competitvely based on data from Pokemon Showdown highest rated replays. This data is curated through use of the Apriori Algorithm.

The plan is for the backend to be python and to utilize a mongoDB database. This project has been a tool for learning about Machine Learning, as currently the learning model being used is a mix of unsupervised and associative learning.

The groundwork for this project has been laid out. It's currently on hiatus until I make more time for it.
